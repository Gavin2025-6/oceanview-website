How It Works

A documented path from target vehicle to shipping coordination.

The process is designed for buyers who need clarity before committing: target vehicle requirements, matched supply, deposit reservation, pickup, verified vehicle details, invoice, and storage coordination.

Buyer shares the target vehicle requirements.

Model, trim, color, package, budget, timing, destination, and Canada-only requirements can be included from the start.

Oceanview sources available vehicle supply.

Canada inventory is prioritized first. U.S. supply can be considered where the deal structure and title timing fit the buyer requirement.

Buyer confirms and places deposit.

Once a unit matches the request, the buyer reserves it with a deposit so the vehicle can be secured.

Vehicle is picked up.

Pickup is coordinated after reservation and confirmation of the unit details.

We share photos, VIN, build date, and inspection details.

The buyer receives practical documentation before final settlement, including real vehicle photos and key identifying details for the matched unit.

Invoice is issued.

Final payment is handled against the invoice after the buyer reviews the verified vehicle details.

Vehicle is stored and shipping is coordinated.

The vehicle is moved into the warehouse or storage path and shipping coordination begins for the buyer’s preferred destination.

Buyer Documents

Request the details that matter before shipment.

Real vehicle photos, available price, VIN, build date, package details, and inspection notes keep each offer grounded and easy to review.
