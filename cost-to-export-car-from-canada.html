Export cost guide

How much does it cost to export a car from Canada?

The total cost depends on the vehicle, Canadian location, destination country, shipping method, documents, and local import charges. Oceanview Auto helps buyers understand the cost stack before committing.

Request a sourcing estimate

Quick answer

There is no single fixed export cost. A real estimate should include the vehicle purchase price, local transport, inspection, export documentation, freight, insurance, destination charges, duties, taxes, and any local compliance or registration work.

Cost components

Information needed for an estimate

  1. Target vehicle model and year range.
  2. Destination country and port.
  3. New, used, clean title, or special condition requirements.
  4. Approximate budget and quantity.
  5. Preferred shipping method if known.
