This policy explains what information Oceanview Auto collects when you use this website or submit a vehicle sourcing request, and how that information is used and protected.

01Information We Collect

When you submit a request through our contact form or WhatsApp, we collect the details you provide — name, email address, phone number, target vehicle information, and destination market. We do not collect payment information through this website.

02How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to respond to sourcing requests, prepare vehicle offers, and communicate with you about your inquiry. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

03Sharing of Information

Information may be shared with logistics, shipping, or documentation partners only where necessary to complete a vehicle export transaction you have requested, and only with your knowledge.

04Cookies & Analytics

This site may use basic analytics tools to understand general traffic patterns. These tools do not collect information that personally identifies you beyond standard technical data such as browser type and approximate location.

05Data Retention

We retain inquiry information only as long as needed to respond to your request or as required for our business records.

06Contact

If you have questions about this policy or would like your information removed from our records, please reach out through our contact page.
