Quick answer

To export a car from Canada to Dubai, the buyer needs a suitable vehicle, verified ownership documents, Canadian export handling, ocean freight, UAE import clearance, and local registration or resale preparation. Oceanview Auto focuses on sourcing and preparing vehicles for export buyers.

Export process

  1. Confirm the target model, year, condition, budget, and destination port.
  2. Source and inspect suitable Canadian inventory.
  3. Verify title, ownership, lien status, and export eligibility.
  4. Prepare inland transport to the departure port.
  5. Arrange container or RoRo shipping where available.
  6. Coordinate documents for UAE import clearance.
